dQueue

General purpose queuing tool that lives on the Ethereum blockchain

Transparent and fair queuing of people and assets

DQUEUE leavs on the Ethereum Blockchin. This allows that every queuing process (adding to queue, waiting order and processing) can be easely checked and validated. By respecting first come first serve contract, fairnes in processing order is guarrantied.
